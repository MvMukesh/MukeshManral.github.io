LinkLens:

The Missing Link Between Your Digital Content and Your Notebook Shell

LinkLens is a revolutionary new tool that allows you to embed any digital content in your notebook shell. This includes images, videos, audio, and more. LinkLens makes it easy to create and share rich, interactive notebooks that are more engaging and informative than ever before.
